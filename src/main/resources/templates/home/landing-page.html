Student

As a student you will be able to
  • View Available
  • Apply to Courses
  • View grades and Attendance
  • View Credited Courses

Instructor

As a instructor you will be able to
  • Update/Manage Curricula
  • Manage Classes
  • Post Grades/Attendance
  • Add Students to a Course
  • Access Dynamic Rosters

Coordinators

As a coordinator you will be able to
  • Manage your Users
  • Manage Curricula
  • Assign Courses to Instructors
  • Manage Instructors
